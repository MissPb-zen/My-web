Zarina Andreeva is a Russian-born writer.
Her books range from children's books to macabre fantasy and thrillers. Most of the books are illustrated by the author herself, using delicate, light watercolors, adding a magical quality to the characters.

__

To immerse yourself in the worlds of Zarina Andreeva means experiencing a spectrum of emotions: romance, kindness, anxiety and suspense.
